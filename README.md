**AirGuard** is an AI-powered, intelligent air-quality guidance platform designed for highly polluted cities in India.

Instead of just displaying complex numbers and colors like existing applications (eg: Maps, Weather App), AirGuard focuses on one **Simple GOAL:** Telling people exactly what to do to stay safe. 

It gathers real time AQI and weather data, utilizing Machine Learning model to forecast future air quality up to 7 days in Advance. By understanding both environmental conditions and personal health profiles of user, the platform provides **Highly personalized**, practical actions - such as when to go outside, what precautions to take, and how to plan daily activities etc.  

**AirGuard** bridges the gap between raw data and action, transforming awareness into real-world impact by empowering citizens with a Personalized Predictive Decision-Support System.
